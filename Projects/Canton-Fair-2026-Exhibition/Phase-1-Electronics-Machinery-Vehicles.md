| Category | Key Products | Product Development Potential |

| AI Factory Solutions | Predictive maintenance, quality inspection, LLM copilots | Management process enhancement |
| Smart Logistics | Automated warehousing, sorting machines, conveyor systems | Supply chain optimization |

### Key 2026 Trends
- **Agentic AI** — Systems that autonomously diagnose and execute production solutions
- **LLM Factory Copilots** — Interest surged from 16% to 35% (used for knowledge management)
- **Cobots** — 70% of orders now from non-automotive sectors, ideal for SMEs
- **5G Industrial Connectivity** — Boosting equipment connectivity by ~30%, cutting downtime by ~40%

### What to Look For

- [ ] AI-powered quality inspection systems
- [ ] 5G industrial routers and factory connectivity platforms
- [ ] Predictive maintenance and equipment management software
- [ ] Digital twin solutions for production planning
- [ ] Automated guided vehicles (AGVs) for warehouse operations
- [ ] LLM-based knowledge management and worker copilot tools
