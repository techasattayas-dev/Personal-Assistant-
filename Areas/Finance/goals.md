## Goal Setting Framework

### SMART Goals
All goals should be:
- **S**pecific: Clearly defined
- **M**easurable: Track progress with numbers
- **A**chievable: Realistic given income/expenses
- **R**elevant: Aligned with values and priorities
- **T**ime-bound: Has a deadline

### Priority Matrix

| Priority | Goals |
|----------|-------|
| High | Emergency fund, Debt payoff |
| Medium | Down payment, Retirement |
| Low | Vacation fund, Luxury purchases |
