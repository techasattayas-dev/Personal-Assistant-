# Health Area

**Area Responsibility**: Maintaining physical and mental health, fitness, nutrition, and overall wellness.

## Overview

This area contains health tracking, fitness goals, medical records references, and wellness practices.

## Files in This Area

- **[goals.md](./goals.md)** - Health and fitness goals
- **tracking.md** (create as needed) - Daily health metrics
- **medical.md** (create as needed) - Medical history and appointments
- **fitness.md** (create as needed) - Workout routines and progress

## Key Health Areas

### Physical Health
- Exercise and fitness
- Sleep quality and quantity
- Nutrition and diet
- Medical checkups

### Mental Health
- Stress management
- Mindfulness and meditation
- Work-life balance
- Mental health support

### Preventive Care
- Annual checkups
- Dental visits
- Vision exams
- Vaccinations

## Review Frequency

**Daily**: Log health metrics (optional)
**Weekly**: Review exercise and sleep patterns
**Monthly**: Assess progress toward health goals
**Annual**: Comprehensive health review, schedule checkups
