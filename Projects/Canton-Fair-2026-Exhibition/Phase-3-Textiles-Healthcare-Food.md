# Phase 3: Textiles, Healthcare & Food

**Dates:** May 1–5, 2026 (Spring) | October 31–November 4, 2026 (Autumn)
**Location:** Areas A, B & C, China Import and Export Fair Complex, Guangzhou

---

## Section Overview

Phase 3 covers **textiles, clothing, personal care, healthcare, toys, sports, and food products**. This phase is key for consumer lifestyle brands, health & wellness product development, and food industry sourcing.
