## 🗣️ ESSENTIAL JAPANESE PHRASES

### Ordering Food:
- **Sumimasen** (すみません) = "Excuse me" / "Thank you"
- **Kore kudasai** (これください) = "This one, please" (point at menu)
- **Mizu kudasai** (水ください) = "Water, please"
- **Oishii!** (おいしい) = "Delicious!"

### Paying:
- **Okaikei onegaishimasu** (お会計お願いします) = "Check, please"
- **Ikura desu ka?** (いくらですか) = "How much?"

### After Meal:
- **Gochisousama deshita** (ごちそうさまでした) = "Thank you for the meal"

### Useful:
- **Eigo no menu arimasu ka?** (英語のメニューありますか) = "English menu available?"
- **Shashin wa daijoubu desu ka?** (写真は大丈夫ですか) = "Can I take a photo?"

## 🚫 DINING ETIQUETTE - DO'S & DON'TS

### ✅ DO:
- ✅ Use oshibori (hot towel) before eating
- ✅ Slurp noodles (shows appreciation!)
- ✅ Say "itadakimasu" before eating
- ✅ Say "gochisousama" after eating
- ✅ Remove shoes if required
- ✅ Wait to be seated (don't seat yourself)

### ❌ DON'T:
- ❌ Stick chopsticks upright in rice (funeral ritual)
- ❌ Pass food chopstick-to-chopstick (funeral ritual)
- ❌ Point chopsticks at people
- ❌ Tip (seriously, don't!)
- ❌ Talk loudly on phone in restaurant
- ❌ Walk and eat on street (rude in traditional areas)

## 📸 FOOD PHOTO TIPS

### When to Ask Permission:
- ✅ **Upscale restaurants** - Always ask first
- ✅ **Traditional places** - Ask if unsure
- ⚠️ **Casual places** - Usually OK, be quick
- ✅ **Markets** - OK for food, ask for vendors

### How to Ask:
**"Shashin wa daijoubu desu ka?"** (写真は大丈夫ですか)
= "Is it okay to take a photo?"

### Photography Etiquette:
- 📸 Take photos BEFORE eating
- 📸 No flash in dim restaurants
- 📸 Quick shots, don't hold up service
- 📸 Photos of food OK, people require permission
