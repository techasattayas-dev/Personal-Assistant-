## Company Overview

Airports of Thailand Public Company Limited (AOT) is a state-owned enterprise that operates **six major international airports** in Thailand, accounting for over **90% of Thailand's air traffic**:

| Airport | Location |
|---------|----------|
| Suvarnabhumi (BKK) | Bangkok |
| Don Mueang (DMK) | Bangkok |
| Phuket (HKT) | Phuket |
| Chiang Mai (CNX) | Chiang Mai |
| Hat Yai (HDY) | Songkhla |
| Mae Fah Luang-Chiang Rai (CEI) | Chiang Rai |

Business segments include airport management, hotel business, ground aviation services, security services, and perishable goods project management.
