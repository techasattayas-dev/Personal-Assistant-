# Canton Fair 2026 Exhibition — Product Development & Management Research

## Objective

Bring management team to the Canton Fair (China Import and Export Fair) in Guangzhou, China to explore product categories, discover innovation trends, and gather ideas for product development and management process enhancement.

## Context

The Canton Fair is China's largest and most influential trade exhibition, held biannually in Guangzhou since 1957. It features 30,000+ exhibitors across 1.55 million sqm of exhibition space, attracting 200,000+ international buyers from 211 countries. The fair is divided into **3 phases**, each covering different product sectors.

## Exhibition Overview

**Venue:** China Import and Export Fair Complex, No. 382 Yuejiang Zhong Road, Haizhu District, Guangzhou

### Spring 2026 (139th Session)

| Phase | Dates | Focus Areas |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Phase 1** | April 15–19, 2026 | Electronics, Machinery, Vehicles & Energy |
| **Phase 2** | April 23–27, 2026 | Consumer Goods, Home Decor, Gifts & Building |
| **Phase 3** | May 1–5, 2026 | Textiles, Healthcare, Food & Personal Care |

### Autumn 2026 (140th Session)

| Phase | Dates | Focus Areas |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Phase 1** | October 15–19, 2026 | Electronics, Machinery & Hardware |
| **Phase 2** | October 23–27, 2026 | Consumer Goods, Gifts & Textiles |
| **Phase 3** | October 31–Nov 4, 2026 | Clothing, Food & Health Products |

**Hours:** 9:30 AM – 6:00 PM daily

## Resources

### Registration
- Official website: https://www.cantonfair.org.cn/en-US
- Online registration is **free** (on-site badge costs 100 RMB / ~$15 USD)
- Canton Fair APP available for 365-day catalog access

### Getting There
- **Metro:** Line 3 from Baiyun Airport → Tiyu Xi Station → Transfer Line 8 → Xingang Dong or Pazhou Station (~43 min)
- Venue is on Pazhou Island, Haizhu District

### Key Stats
- 30,000+ exhibitors (35% are national high-tech / specialized enterprises)
- ~3,000 Canton Fair brand exhibitors
- Nearly 70% of overseas buyers from Belt and Road Initiative nations
