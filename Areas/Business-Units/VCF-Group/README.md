# VCF Group — KPI Dashboard

## Business Overview
- **Entity**: VCF Group (Thailand)
- **Operations**: Pig farming + feed production
- **Facilities**: 4 feedmills, multiple pig farms
- **Key Products**: Animal feed, live pigs, breeding stock

## KPI Categories

### Production
- Feed output (tons/day)
- Feed conversion ratio (FCR)
- Pig inventory (total head)
- Mortality rate (%)
- Average daily gain — ADG (g/day)
- Farrowing rate (%)

### Financial
- Revenue (THB)
- COGS (THB)
- Gross margin (%)
- Feed cost per kg (THB)
- Operating expenses (THB)

### Operational
- Feedmill utilization (%)
- Equipment uptime (%)
- Biosecurity compliance score (%)

### HR
- Headcount
- Productivity — pigs per employee

## Data Input
Place monthly CSV files in `data/` folder with format: `kpi-YYYY-MM.csv`
