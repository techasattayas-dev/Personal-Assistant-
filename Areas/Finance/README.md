# Finance Area

**Area Responsibility**: Managing personal finances, budgeting, expense tracking, and financial goal achievement.

## Overview

This area contains all financial tracking, budgeting, and goal-related documents. Use this as your central hub for understanding and managing your financial health.

## Files in This Area

- **[budget.md](./budget.md)** - Monthly and annual budget with category allocations
- **[expenses.md](./expenses.md)** - Transaction log with detailed expense tracking
- **[investments.md](./investments.md)** - Investment portfolio tracking and analysis
- **[goals.md](./goals.md)** - Financial goals, milestones, and progress tracking

## Tracking Categories

### Expenses
- Groceries
- Utilities (electricity, water, gas, internet)
- Healthcare (insurance, medical, prescriptions)
- Transportation (gas, public transit, car maintenance)
- Entertainment (streaming, hobbies, events)
- Dining (restaurants, takeout, coffee)
- Shopping (clothing, electronics, household items)
- Education (courses, books, learning materials)

### Income
- Salary
- Freelance/Side Income
- Investment Returns
- Other Income

## Monthly Review Process

1. **Update Budget** (1st of month)
   - Review last month's spending
   - Adjust current month's allocations
   - Set savings targets

2. **Categorize Expenses** (ongoing)
   - Log all transactions in expenses.md
   - Use financial template for consistency
   - Attach receipts when necessary

3. **Review Progress** (end of month)
   - Compare actual vs. budget
   - Analyze spending patterns
   - Identify areas for improvement

## Integration

- **Notion**: Syncs to Finance database
- **Google Sheets**: Optional export to CSV
- **Banking**: Manual entry (for security)

## Best Practices

1. **Log transactions daily** - Don't let them pile up
2. **Review weekly** - Quick check of spending patterns
3. **Monthly deep dive** - Full budget vs. actual analysis
4. **Keep receipts** - For significant purchases and tax items
5. **Tag tax deductibles** - Makes tax time easier

### Check Financial Health
Review these key metrics monthly:
- Savings rate (% of income saved)
- Budget adherence (spending vs. budget)
- Net worth trend
- Goal progress
