# Phase 2: Consumer Goods, Home Decor & Building Materials

**Dates:** April 23–27, 2026 (Spring) | October 23–27, 2026 (Autumn)
**Location:** Areas A, B & C, China Import and Export Fair Complex, Guangzhou

---

## Section Overview

Phase 2 focuses on **consumer products, home furnishing, gifts, and building materials**. This phase is ideal for discovering retail product ideas, home & lifestyle innovations, and construction/renovation solutions.
