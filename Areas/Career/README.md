# Career Area

**Area Responsibility**: Professional development, career planning, skills development, and work-related goals.

## Overview

This area contains career goals, professional development tracking, resume/portfolio materials, and work-related planning.

## Files in This Area

- **[goals.md](./goals.md)** - Career goals and professional development
- **skills.md** (create as needed) - Skills inventory and learning plan
- **achievements.md** (create as needed) - Career accomplishments and wins
- **networking.md** (create as needed) - Professional contacts and relationships

## Key Career Areas

### Professional Development
- Skills to develop
- Certifications and courses
- Conference attendance
- Reading and learning

### Career Planning
- Short-term goals (1 year)
- Medium-term goals (3-5 years)
- Long-term vision (10+ years)
- Job search if applicable

### Performance
- Work achievements
- Performance reviews
- Promotions and raises
- Projects and responsibilities

## Review Frequency

**Weekly**: Review current work projects and priorities
**Monthly**: Assess progress on career goals
**Quarterly**: Update resume and portfolio
**Annual**: Comprehensive career review and planning
