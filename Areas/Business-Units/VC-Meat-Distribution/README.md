# VC Meat Distribution Co., Ltd.

**Integrated Fresh Food Retail & Distribution Network**

---

## 1. Company Overview

VC Meat Distribution Co., Ltd. is the retail and distribution arm of VCF Group, one of Thailand's leading integrated pork producers. The company operates a growing network of fresh food retail outlets under the **Freshmart** concept, delivering high-quality pork, poultry, and essential food products directly to consumers.

Leveraging VCF Group's vertically integrated supply chain — from feed production and pig farming to slaughtering and processing — VC Meat Distribution ensures consistent quality, traceability, and competitive pricing.

**Mission**: Provide safe, fresh, and affordable food while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene, animal welfare, and supply chain transparency.

---

## 2. Business Model

VC Meat Distribution operates under a **Farm-to-Retail integrated model**, allowing the company to control quality and cost throughout the supply chain.

### Upstream Integration
- Pig farms operated by VCF Group
- Feed production facilities
- Slaughterhouse and primary processing

### Downstream Distribution
- Fresh meat retail stores (Freshmart)
- Distribution to restaurants and local food operators
- Wholesale supply to small retailers

This structure allows VC Meat Distribution to deliver fresh products daily with stable pricing and strict quality control.
