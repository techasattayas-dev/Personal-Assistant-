## Calendar Best Practices

1. **Time Blocking**: Schedule deep work time
2. **Buffer Time**: Add 5-10 minutes between meetings
3. **Review Time**: Protect daily/weekly review slots
4. **Focus Time**: Block time for important projects
5. **Personal Time**: Schedule self-care and breaks
