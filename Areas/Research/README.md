# Research Area

**Area Responsibility**: Market research, competitive analysis, technology research, and feasibility studies for business opportunities and projects.

## Overview

This area contains research findings, competitive analysis, market studies, and technical research for new business opportunities and ongoing projects.

## Research Categories

### 1. Market Research
- Market size and trends
- Customer analysis
- Demand forecasting
- Pricing analysis
- Market entry strategies

### 2. Competitive Analysis
- Competitor benchmarking
- Best practices from industry leaders (CP Group, Betagro, Tyson Foods)
- Competitive positioning
- SWOT analysis

### 3. Technology Research
- Equipment specifications
- Automation systems
- Industry 4.0 technologies
- Software solutions (ERP, farm management)
- Process innovations

### 4. Vendor & Supplier Research
- Equipment suppliers
- Technical specifications
- Pricing comparison
- Vendor capabilities
- References and case studies

### 5. Regulatory & Standards
- Food safety standards (HACCP, GMP, ISO)
- Export requirements (Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia)
- Environmental regulations
- Building codes and standards
- Certifications needed

### 6. Feasibility Studies
- New business opportunities (pet food, edible oil, amino acids, dairy)
- Expansion projects
- Technology adoption
- Market entry analysis

## Research Workflow

### 1. Define Research Question
- What do we need to know?
- Why is this important?
- What decisions depend on this?

### 2. Gather Information
- Web search for latest information
- Industry reports and publications
- Competitor websites and materials
- Technical specifications
- Expert consultations

### 3. Analyze Data
- Compare and contrast findings
- Identify patterns and trends
- Calculate costs and ROI
- Assess risks and opportunities

### 4. Document Findings
- Create research document
- Use structured format
- Include data sources
- Add visual aids (tables, charts)

### 5. Make Recommendations
- Actionable next steps
- Decision framework
- Implementation considerations

### 6. Review & Update
- Quarterly review of research
- Update with new information
- Archive outdated research

## Research Examples

### Example 1: Pet Food Manufacturing Research
**Question**: Is pet food manufacturing viable for VCF Group?

**Key Areas**:
- Market size (ASEAN, China, Japan)
- OEM opportunities
- Equipment requirements
- Raw material sourcing (using pork by-products)
- Regulatory requirements
- Competitor analysis

### Example 2: Feedmill Equipment Research
**Question**: Best supplier for 60 tons/hour feedmill pelleting system?

**Key Areas**:
- Technical specifications
- Vendor comparison (CPM, Andritz, Bühler)
- Pricing and lead times
- After-sales support
- References from similar installations

### Example 3: Export Market Research
**Question**: What's required to export pork to Singapore?

**Key Areas**:
- Import regulations
- Certification requirements (AVA approval)
- Cold chain logistics
- Pricing benchmarks
- Competitor analysis
