- **Comprehensive coverage**: Multiple sources in one query
- **Up-to-date**: Current as of search time

## Perplexity Integration Workflow

### Setup
1. **Subscribe to Perplexity Pro** (recommended for deeper research)
2. **Create Collections** in Perplexity:
   - Thailand Agribusiness
   - Myanmar Agriculture
   - Technology & Innovation
   - Competitors
   - Export Markets
   - New Ventures (Pet Food, Dairy, etc.)

### Daily Workflow (Morning - 15-20 minutes)

**Step 1**: Run morning Perplexity queries (6 queries above)

**Step 2**: Copy relevant findings to daily brief template

**Step 3**: Categorize by priority:
- 🚨 Priority (Action required)
- 📊 Monitor (Keep track)
- 💡 Opportunity (Explore later)
- 📚 Deep dive (Read when time allows)

**Step 4**: Add to Inbox if action needed:
```
- Follow up: New feedmill technology mentioned in news
- Research: CP Group expanding into pet food (competitive intel)
- Contact: Supplier mentioned in article about new equipment
```

**Step 5**: Save daily brief to `News-Intelligence/daily/`

### Weekly Workflow (Sunday - 30 minutes)

**Step 1**: Review all 7 daily briefs from the week

**Step 2**: Run weekly Perplexity deep dive queries

**Step 3**: Identify trends and patterns

**Step 4**: Create weekly intelligence report

**Step 5**: Add strategic action items to weekly review

### Monthly Workflow (1st of month - 1 hour)

**Step 1**: Review all weekly reports from previous month

**Step 2**: Run monthly Perplexity queries

**Step 3**: Create monthly intelligence summary

**Step 4**: Update Areas (Research, Projects) based on intelligence

**Step 5**: Present key findings in monthly review
