## TECHNOLOGY & INNOVATION

### AI-Powered Livestock Robot Launch
**Nuwa Agricultural Technology** unveiled its "Cleaning + AI Monitoring" livestock robot at Silicon Valley (March 2026). Three-layer architecture: autonomous robots, subscription analytics platform, and growing dataset from real-world environments. Livestock farming remains one of the **least digitized sectors** globally.

### Industry Shift: "Decision Agriculture"
USDA's National Institute of Food and Agriculture describes the transition from "precision ag" to **"decision ag"** - AI-driven farming that optimizes rather than just measures. Focused AI adoption correlates with **up to 20% higher yields**.

### Automatic Milking & Farm Automation
- Advanced milking systems and automated feeding gaining adoption
- Wearable devices for animal health, behavior, and reproduction tracking
- Machine learning predicting illness and optimizing feeding schedules

### Market Growth

- AI in agriculture market: **USD 1.7B (2023) to USD 4.7B (2028)**
