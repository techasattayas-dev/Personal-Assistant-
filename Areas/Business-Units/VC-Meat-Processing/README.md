# VC Meat Processing Co., Ltd.

**Company Profile**

---

## 1. Company Overview

VC Meat Processing Co., Ltd. is a modern pork processing company in Thailand specializing in slaughtering, cutting, processing, and distribution of high-quality pork products.

The company operates as part of VCF Group, an integrated agribusiness organization with operations covering:
- Livestock feed production
- Pig farming
- Pork processing
- Food distribution and retail

Through vertical integration, VC Meat ensures full traceability, strict food safety standards, and consistent product quality from farm to consumer.

VC Meat supplies pork products to both domestic markets and international customers, with a focus on reliability, hygiene, and sustainable food production.

---

## 2. Business Structure

VC Meat operates within the integrated supply chain of VCF Group.

### Upstream
- Pig farms under VCF Group
- Controlled breeding and animal welfare management
- Feed supplied from VCF feed mills

### Midstream
- Slaughterhouse operations
- Meat cutting and processing
- Food safety and quality assurance systems

### Downstream
- Distribution to wholesalers
- Retail through VC Meat Distribution / Freshmart stores
- Supply to restaurants, food service operators, and processors

This structure ensures stable supply, traceability, and strict quality control across the entire value chain.

## 4. Food Safety & Quality Systems

VC Meat maintains strict food safety management systems based on internationally recognized standards.

The company operates a **Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point (HACCP)** system to identify, evaluate, and control food safety hazards throughout the production process.

Key systems include:
- **HACCP** Food Safety Management
- **GMP** (Good Manufacturing Practices)
- **GHP** (Good Hygiene Practices)
- Process monitoring and verification
- Hazard analysis covering biological, chemical, and physical risks

These systems ensure products meet both national and international food safety requirements.
